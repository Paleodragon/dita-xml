Sum Up

How to Sum Up Numbers

This list of steps will show you how to use the sum up feature in the JPS Calculator app.

To successfully sum up numbers you should understand the concept of addition.
  1. Enter a number
  2. Click on the plus (+) symbol.
  3. Enter a second number.
  4. Choose the equality (=) symbol.
The result of the numbers that were added together will be shown.
You want to sum up the numbers 3 and 5. The equation will appear as 3 + 5 = 8. Enter the number 3. Then, press the plus (+) symbol. Enter the number 5. Choose the equality (=) symbol. The result appears on the screen.

What is Addition?

Here we will learn about the concept of addition.

Addition is the process of adding one number to another.

To add, the (+) symbol must be used.

Subtract

How to Subtract Numbers

Here you will find steps showing how to use the subtract feature on the JPS calculator app

To successfully subtract numbers, you must first understand the concept of subtraction.

  1. Enter a number.
  2. Click on the (-) button.
  3. Enter a second number.
  4. Choose the (=) button.
The subtraction result will be displayed.
You want to subtract 2 from 3. The equation will appear as 3 - 2 = 3. Enter the number 3. Press the minus symbol (-). Enter 2. Then, press the equality symbol (=).The result will appear on the screen.

What is Subtraction?

Here we learn what subtraction means.

Subtraction is the process of taking one number or amount away from another.

To subtract a number from another number you must use the minus symbol.
