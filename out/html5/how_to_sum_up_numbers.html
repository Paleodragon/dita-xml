How to Sum Up Numbers

This list of steps will show you how to use the sum up feature in the JPS Calculator app.

To successfully sum up numbers you should understand the concept of addition.
  1. Enter a number
  2. Click on the plus (+) symbol.
  3. Enter a second number.
  4. Choose the equality (=) symbol.
The result of the numbers that were added together will be shown.
You want to sum up the numbers 3 and 5. The equation will appear as 3 + 5 = 8. Enter the number 3. Then, press the plus (+) symbol. Enter the number 5. Choose the equality (=) symbol. The result appears on the screen.
