How to Multiply Numbers

Here you will find steps showing how to use the multiply feature on the JPS calculator app

To successfully multiply numbers, you must first understand the concept of multiplication.

  1. Enter a number.
  2. Click on the (*) button.
  3. Enter a second number.
  4. Choose the (=) button.
The multiplication result will be displayed.
You want to multiply 2 times 7. The equation will appear as 2 * 7 = 14. Enter the number 2. Press the multiply symbol (*). Enter 7. Then, press the equality symbol (=).The result will appear on the screen.
