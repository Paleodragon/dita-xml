How to Divide Numbers

Here you will find steps showing how to use the division feature on the JPS calculator app

To successfully divide numbers, you must first understand the concept of division.

  1. Enter a number.
  2. Click on the (/) button.
  3. Enter a second number.
  4. Choose the (=) button.
The division result will be displayed.
You want to divide the number 6 by 2. The equation will appear as 6 / 2 = 3. Enter the number 6. Press the division symbol (/). Enter 2. Then, press the equality symbol (=).The result will appear on the screen.
